About Me

Dushyanta is a Master’s student in the Computer Science Department at The Ohio State University. He is currently working under the supervision of Prof. Huan Sun on Weakly Supervised methods in Natural Language Processing. In Summer 2017, he worked as an SDE intern at the AWS Deep Learning group in Seattle that released the AWS Comprehend tool. Prior to joining OSU he was a Research Assistant at the Ubiquitous Knowledge Processing Lab under the supervision of Prof Dr. Iryna Gurevych.
